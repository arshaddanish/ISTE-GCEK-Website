TED X GCEK

TED X GCEK

TEDx GCE Kannur was conducted by the ISTE GCEK Chapter on 21st October 2018. The event sought out the unconventional thinkers leading their field to new frontiers and brought out their genius before the limelight. The event intertwined the cold and rational thought with the hues and tones of creativity. The students were inspired by the speakers to tackle unfamiliar and complex problems and to meet the challenges set out by them.The audience was enraptured by the peppy and vibrant country folk songs sung by the Pune based folk/rock country band After Acoustics. Arpita Khadria wove a well of ideas on stage and provided a model for a perfect work-life balance. The synchronised flow of the hands, feet and emotion was portrayed on stage by Devika Sajeevan, an exceptional dancer who has danced her way to several accolades and achievements with her flowing elegance and refined aesthetic movements. The event was conducted by a vibrant and energetic team led by event organizer Atthri Anand and co-organizer Arunima Das. The team was stacked with talent, desire, and passion. Each member provided something unique and marked their contribution. TEDx GCE Kannur played a pivotal role in soldering the identity of ISTE GCEK Chapter. The event was one of the events that manifested the ideas of our student chapter and the strength and the vigour of the students behind it.
